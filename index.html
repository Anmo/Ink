Welcome to Ink

Ink is a set of tools for quick development of web interfaces.

It offers a fluid and responsive grid, common interface elements, interactive components, a design-first approach with ease of use and simplicity at its core. Start integrating Ink in your projects and remove the hassle of building the basics, staying free to focus on what's important.

Ink is now V2!

Since launch, we've been working hard to make Ink even better. We improved and simplified our grid, made our code easier to learn, made our JS components simpler to implement and rewrote our documentation from scratch.

Check the changelog to learn all about what's new.

Why use Ink?

Fluid

This is undoubtedly one of Ink's biggest advantages. Your layouts will be truly fluid thanks to the way our grid works.

Responsive

Ink is responsive, using a very logical size progression which is dead simple to write into your markup from the get go.

Simple

Ink tries to rely as much as possible on natural language which just makes sense, making it easy to learn and memorize.

Growing

Ink is made with passion. Expect improvements, new solutions and examples, new JS modules as our team keeps building up Ink.
