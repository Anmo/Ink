Displays off-canvas (to the left and to the right) content which can be triggered by clicking elements with the 'left-drawer-trigger' and 'right-drawer-trigger', respectively. The left drawer has the 'left-drawer' class, and the right drawer has the 'right-drawer' class. The content drawer (EG your `
