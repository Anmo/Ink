Ink.UI.Drawer_1 version: 1

Displays off-canvas content which can be triggered by clicking elements with the 'left-drawer-trigger' and 'right-drawer-trigger', respectively. The left drawer has the 'left-drawer' class, and the right drawer has the 'right-drawer' class. The content drawer (EG your `

`) must have the 'content-drawer' class. For more, see the example below, or try the sample.
